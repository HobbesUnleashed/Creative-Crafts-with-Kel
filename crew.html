Becoming part of Stampin' Up! has never been easier or more exciting!


Whether you craft for pleasure and want to buy Stampin’ Up! products at discounted demonstrator prices or you would like to grow your hobby and begin a new business venture, Stampin' Up! offer a great deal to purchase a kit of products to get you started.

Starter kits are chosen entirely by you to suit your crafting ways and desires and you can choose anything from the current catalogues.

How much does it cost?


To purchase your Stampin' Up! products as part of a Starter Kit, you usually pay £99 and choose £130 worth of product!

If you are interested in joining Stampin' Up! either click the button below or follow these easy steps.


  1. Visit the Stampin' Up! website and select join in the tool bar at the top
  2. When you are on the Stampin' Up! join page, scroll down and select the "Join Stampin' Up" box
  3. You will be prompted to enter your email address on the next page, and choose a Demonstrator to sign up with. If my name isn't showing already simply search for me!
  4. On the next page you will need to read and agree to the Independent Demonstrator Agreement (it's nothing scary I promise)
  5. Once you have done that, you will move into the ordering section where you will get to enter all the product details that you would like included in your starter kit. It needs to total no more than £130 and shipping is totally FREE on our Starter Kits too!

Once you have completed the steps above, you will receive a welcome email from Stampin' Up! with a password so that you can log into the Demonstrator website. Within this website you will find everything you need to know about placing your next order, checking the exclusive Demonstrator promotions and so much more.

If you have any questions, at any time, please do not hesitate to contact me.
